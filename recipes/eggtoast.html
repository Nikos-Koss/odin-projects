Egg Toast recipe

Description

A really tasteful dish with basically everything you need for a good breakfast. Eggs cheese mixed together and some green toppings!!

Ingredients
  • 5 Eggs
  • Greens
  • 2 toast slices
  • mayo, honey mustard, a little of olive oil and lemon juice

Steps
  1. Firstly roast mixed eggs with some cheese in a hot pan with butter.
  2. Then add some curry powder,a little salt and pepper.
  3. In the meantime get your toast ready by grilling it in the toaster.
  4. By the time that eggs and toasts are ready, start making your sauce. Take a bowl and add mayo, honey mustard, a little of olive oil and lemon juice
